Superstore Sales Performance & Profitability Dashboard

Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, product categories, and regional trends using the Superstore dataset.

The dashboard transforms raw business data into meaningful insights to support data-driven decision-making and business growth strategies.

Tools Used
Power BI Desktop

Dashboard Structure

Page 1: Sales Overview
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Total Quantity
- Average Discount
- Sales by Region
- Sales by Category
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Segment

Page 2: Profit Analysis
- Profit by Region
- Top 10 Profitable Products
- Profit by Segment
- Profit by Sub-Category
- Monthly Profit Trend

Page 3: Business Insights & Strategic Recommendations
- Key Business Insights
- Strategic Recommendations

Key Findings
- The West region generated the highest sales and profit.
- Technology products were the major revenue drivers.
- Consumer customers contributed the largest share of profit.
- Copiers, Phones, and Accessories were the most profitable sub-categories.
- Tables and Bookcases generated losses and require attention.
- Profit performance improved significantly during the final quarter of the year.

Business Recommendations
- Increase focus on high-profit products and categories.
- Optimize pricing and discount strategies.
- Improve performance of underperforming sub-categories.
- Expand successful regional strategies into lower-performing markets.
- Strengthen customer retention programs.
- Prepare inventory and marketing campaigns before peak-demand periods.
